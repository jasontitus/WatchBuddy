WatchAI Support

About

WatchAI is a voice assistant for Apple Watch. Tap the microphone, ask a question, and hear an AI-powered spoken response directly on your wrist.

Requirements

  • Apple Watch running watchOS 10 or later
  • Internet connection (Wi-Fi or cellular)

How to Use

  1. Open WatchAI on your Apple Watch
  2. Tap the blue microphone button
  3. Ask your question
  4. Tap stop when finished
  5. Listen to the AI response

Troubleshooting

No response after recording: Check that your watch has an internet connection. The app requires a network connection to process your voice.

Microphone permission denied: Go to Settings on your Apple Watch, find WatchAI, and enable microphone access.
