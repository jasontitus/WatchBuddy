WatchBuddy Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Overview

WatchBuddy is a voice assistant app for Apple Watch. Your privacy matters to us, so we designed the app to minimize the data it handles. This policy explains exactly what data leaves your device and where it goes.

Data We Collect

WatchBuddy does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Specifically:

  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks.
  • We do not collect device identifiers, usage statistics, or location data.
  • Your API key is stored securely in the device Keychain and is never sent anywhere except the services described below.

Third-Party Services

WatchBuddy operates in two modes. In both modes, data is processed in real time and is not stored by the app.

Server AI Mode

When "Use Server's AI" is enabled:

  • Your voice recording and access key are sent to the server you configure in Settings.
  • The server handles speech-to-text, AI processing, and text-to-speech entirely on its side.
  • No data is sent directly from your watch to any AI provider.

Bring Your Own Key (BYOK) Mode

When "Use Server's AI" is disabled:

  • Your voice recording is sent to the server you configure for speech-to-text conversion only.
  • The transcribed text is sent directly from your watch to the AI provider you selected, along with your API key.
  • The AI response text is sent back to the server for text-to-speech conversion.

AI Providers

Depending on your settings, your transcribed text may be sent to one of the following third-party AI providers:

  • Google Gemini – Privacy Policy
  • OpenAI – Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic – Privacy Policy

Each provider processes data according to their own privacy policy. We encourage you to review the policy for the provider you choose.

Data Storage

All settings (server URL and AI provider preference) are stored locally on your device using standard watchOS storage. Your API key is stored in the Apple Keychain, which is encrypted and protected by the operating system.

Children's Privacy

WatchBuddy does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the new version will be posted on this page with a revised date.
