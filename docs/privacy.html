WatchAI Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

WatchAI is a voice assistant app for Apple Watch. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Audio Data

When you use the microphone to ask a question, your voice recording is sent to a server for processing. The audio is transcribed to text, a response is generated, and a spoken reply is sent back to your watch. Audio recordings are processed in real time and are not stored permanently on the server.

Data Collection

WatchAI does not collect personal information, create user accounts, or track your activity. No analytics or advertising SDKs are included in the app.

Third-Party Services

The server uses the following services to process your requests:

  • Speech-to-text: Your audio is transcribed locally on the server.
  • AI response generation: The transcribed text is sent to Google Gemini to generate a response.
  • Text-to-speech: The response is converted to audio locally on the server.

Google Gemini's use of data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

Audio recordings and transcriptions are processed transiently and deleted after each request is completed. No conversation history is stored.

Children's Privacy

WatchAI does not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app processes voice queries without creating profiles or storing personal data.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
